Nkhoma University Faculties and Programs

Programmes: Nursing | Education | Theology | Commerce

Courses

Nkhoma University has the following four faculties: Faculty of Commerce , Faculty of Education , Faculty of Nursing and Faculty of Theology

In terms of capacity, currently the Departments have well-trained, highly qualified and experienced academic staff members that can handle the programmes. The facilities include lecture rooms backed up by excellent lighting . Additionally, classrooms are furnished with chairs and desks . Further, the faculties have computers, libraries, offices for staff as well as ICT facilities. Nkhoma University is connected with Internet Services supplied by the Malawi Sustainable Development Network Programme (SDNP) ready to assist students in computer skills and research .
