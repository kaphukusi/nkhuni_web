IN THE NEWS
Nkhoma University (NKHUNI) is a registered and accredited institution of higher learning that offers degrees, diplomas and certificates from the programmes that have been accredited by the Government of Malawi through the National Council of Higher Education.

Nkhoma University Launch

Nkhoma University, of Nkhoma Synod of the Church of Central African Presbyterian (CCAP), was officially launched on 3rd October in Lilongwe. read more

Admission Policy

Nkhoma University is open to students of all faiths and respects academic freedom and freedom of thought and expression within the basic tenets of the Christian faith.

Mission statement

The mission of Nkhoma University is to provide an opportunity for learning to students, enabling them to understand the academic disciplines in a very professional manner and be inspired by the Word of God with its implications for living, character building and service in all God honoring vocations.

Vision statement

The University will provide education, training, research and related services locally,nationally and internationally to support and advance the social, cultural, intellectual and economic development of Malawi
